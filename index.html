How It Works

Allow your location and press locate to pull up restaurants within approximately 10 miles from your location. Pick a restaurant from those provided to you. Check the weather to see if you would like to sit outside. We'll give you the name of the restaurant, an address, a price level on a scale of 1-3 $ and a rating on a scale of 1-5.
